February 28, 2020
On Feb. 28, the W.H.O. raised the coronavirus alert to the highest level.
This comes after more countries confirmed their first cases, with the outbreak reaching as far as sub-Saharan Africa.
Numbers in Europe continue to rise, especially in Italy.
Cases in 14 European countries could be traced back to Italy.
